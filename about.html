Welcome to PIONEER

Pioneer Consultants and Services (PIONEER) is a newly established firm focusing on environmental, safety and training services, such as (but not limited) environmental impact assessment for projects, designing environmental register, environmental management plans, environmental inspection and auditing according to national and international regulations, requirements of Egyptian civil defense and professional training for all previous mentioned items.

  • vision

    Pioneer be a leader in providing its services in tune with the demands and desires of various customers and the best possible picture and to become an expertise house at the regional and international levels and thus extend services according to the best global practices.

  • Mision

    Establish solid business relation and effective cooperation with our clients, which driving PIONEER to work very hard to design tailored consultancy /services suit the specific needs of our clients, sharing with them continually improve your existing health, environmental and/or safety management arrangements.

  • Goal

    • Find the appropriate solutions and services to our clients by the best means of scientific and practical to satisfy their needs.
    • Provide our services to our customers according the best national and international practices.
    • Provide our services to all our customers’ professionalism.
    • Gain the satisfaction of our customers, through precise execution and examine all aspects that meet the needs of work and client.
